Problem

Many people are afraid to speak in public. This is usually natural to everyone. The only way to overcome this fear is to speak in front of a small group then to a large group gradually. However, sometimes it is not easy to find those groups.

Solution

Mira helps by dispaying different amounts of audiences in front of you. Then it records your speech and scans your body language and the tone of your voice. After that, Mira will give a feedback on how to improve your speech.

Technology

Virtual mirrors usually utilize computer vision, face detection, and face tracking technologies to analyze visual patterns and represent digital information. The technology uses algorithms to collect, analyze, and make meaningful inferences from data from one or multiple images.
